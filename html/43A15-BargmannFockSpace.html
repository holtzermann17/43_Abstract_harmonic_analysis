Bargmann-Fock space


The Bargmann-Fock space (or simply Fock space) is the Hilbert space of entire functions, ℱ2⁢(ℂ) s.t.

∫ℂ|F⁢(z)|2⁢e-π⁢|z|2⁢𝑑x⁢𝑑y<∞

with associated inner product

∫ℂF⁢(z)⁢G⁢(z)¯⁢e-π⁢|z|2⁢𝑑x⁢𝑑y

where z=x+i⁢y
